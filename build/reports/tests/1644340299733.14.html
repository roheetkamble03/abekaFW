Digital Assessments

Thank you for choosing digital assessments this year!

Theres one step to take before you starta simple practice test. (Dont worry, its 100% for practice! If this were a real test, wed review your answers to fill-in-the-blank and essay questions to make sure you receive proper credit.) The point of the practice test is to give you a warm-up before a real quiz or test. Youll see the different kinds of questions and how to answer each type. Youll get a tour of the tools and functions you can use, too.

Take My Practice Test required before moving on to available tests/quizzes
