Agreements

Photography/Videography

In the event that a Pensacola Christian College photographer or videographer takes a picture with me in it, either singly or in a group, I give permission for my picture to be used in future brochures, videos, or other publications of Pensacola Christian College, Inc., Abeka Academy, or any of PCCs affiliate corporations. I also agree to abide by the dress requirements (see below) for the duration of graduation week.


Abeka Academy Graduation Dress Requirements


General
Modest business-casual attire is appropriate for guests and graduates on campus. Our preference is for men to wear dress slacks and collared shirts and for ladies to wear knee-length skirts or dresses.

Sports Center
The Sports Center, featuring rock climbing, bowling, water sliding, FlowRider surfing, ice skating, and much more, will be open for you to enjoy during your visit. Clothing appropriate for each activity is required. Please do not wear sweatpants, spandex, undershirts, tank tops, or clothing with inappropriate writing or pictures.

Men: casual shirt; athletic pants, jeans, or shorts (longer lengths); jeans or wind/athletic pants required for rock climbing; trunk-style swimsuit for the water park.

Women: casual shirt; knee-length denim/casual skirt or walking shorts; wind/athletic pants required for rock climbing (optional for ice skating); modest swimsuit that fully covers midriff for the water park.

Official Graduation Ceremony Attire
Since graduation is a formal event, each graduate is expected to maintain an appropriate appearance. In addition to official graduation regalia, students must wear Sunday dress and be properly groomed (see below). Hair style should be such that the graduation cap will fit correctly with no bangs showing. Rehearsal on graduation morning is a full dress rehearsal. For both rehearsal and ceremony, you will be asked to change clothing or trim hair to meet these requirements.

Men:
  • Black or navy dress pants with belt (avoid denim)
  • Solid white dress shirt (top button buttoned)
  • Dark necktie (bolo/bowtie is inappropriate)
  • Black or navy solid-colored socks
  • Black solid-colored dress shoes
  • Facial hair must be neatly trimmed, groomed, and 1/2 inch or shorter in length.
  • Natural-colored hair neatly combed and tapered and may not come over the ears, eyebrows, or collar (no braids, dreadlocks, buns, or ponytails).
  • Necklaces, earrings, or other body jewelry should not be worn.
Women:
  • Black or navy skirt (must be at least knee-length, but should not show below graduation robe; avoid denim)
  • Sleeved, solid-white blouse (if blouse has a collar, it should lie flat under your graduation robe; T-shirts are inappropriate)
  • Black solid-colored dress shoes
  • Long hair needs to be pinned back (wear natural-colored hair).
  • Limit jewelry to a watch, simple rings, and simple earrings (no nose or lip jewelry).
